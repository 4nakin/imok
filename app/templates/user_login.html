I'm OK! is a website and mobile app that allows you to tell your friends and loved ones that you're okay in the event of an emergency or disaster situation.

NOTE: This website is not yet ready for public use.

I'm OK! was rapidly developed as an entrant in the Random Hacks of Kindness codejam.

You can learn more about this project and its goals by looking through this slide deck. If you have any additional questions, you can contact us at [imok AT jeztek.com].
